ABOUT ME


Greetings! I'm Toritseju Samson Moju, a UK-based professional passionate about bringing design to life through web programming. With a B.Sc. in Computer Science and an M.Sc. in Data Analysis for Business Intelligence, I've honed a unique skill set that seamlessly merges design and development.

I've had the privilege of contributing my skills to a diverse range of projects. As a UI/UX designer and web programmer, I've designed interfaces for mobile games that captivate users and ensure an immersive gaming experience. Additionally, I've played a key role in developing HR management systems, fintech applications, and learning management systems, enhancing user engagement and overall satisfaction. My flexible approach to problem-solving and my commitment to staying at the forefront of industry trends make me a valuable asset to any team. I relish the opportunity to collaborate with developers to ensure that designs are not only visually stunning but also function seamlessly.
